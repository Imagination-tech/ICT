Who We Are

Imagination Communication & Technology (ICT Mobile) is a fast-growing mobile phone and ICT service business located in Kobaope, Iwo, Osun State, Nigeria. We specialize in providing high-quality phone repairs, selling reliable used smartphones, and offering essential ICT solutions to individuals, students, and businesses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver affordable, trusted, and professional mobile & ICT services powered by innovation, knowledge, and excellent customer care. We aim to make technology accessible and useful to everyone.
